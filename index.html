Office & Screen Work

Identified Risks

Prolonged exposure to screens causing eye strain and fatigue. Poor posture leading to musculoskeletal disorders, specifically back and neck pain.

Required Protection

Use a fully adjustable ergonomic chair with lumbar support. Ensure the monitor is positioned at eye level. Take a 5-minute break every hour to stand up and walk.

Construction Site

‚Ä∫

Identified Risks

High risk of heavy objects falling from heights, slips, trips, and puncture wounds from sharp debris. Exposure to excessive noise levels.

Required Protection

Mandatory hard hat (helmet) at all times. Steel-toed safety boots with reinforced soles. High visibility jacket or vest. Hearing protection in designated zones.

Gardening & Outdoors

Identified Risks

Cuts from sharp tools or thorns, risk of sunstroke or dehydration, insect bites, and handling of chemical fertilizers.

Required Protection

Cut-resistant gloves. Wide-brimmed hat and sunscreen for UV protection. Thick, reinforced trousers. Safety glasses when using trimmers.

Factory & Machines

‚Ä∫

Identified Risks

Crushing hazards from heavy machinery, entanglement in moving parts, and long-term hearing loss due to constant loud noise.

Required Protection

Industrial-grade ear defenders or earplugs. Impact-resistant safety goggles. Close-fitting clothing (no loose fabric). Steel-capped safety shoes.

Chemical Laboratory

‚Ä∫

Identified Risks

Severe chemical burns from splashes, inhalation of toxic or corrosive vapors, and potential fire hazards.

Required Protection

Long cotton lab coat (buttoned). Chemical-resistant nitrile gloves. Sealed safety goggles. Respiratory mask if ventilation is insufficient.
